# Monday 2026-06-22 · Discipline

> "A soft Monday becomes a heavy week."

Monday does not need a warmup lap. It needs a signal. If the first signal is drift, the whole day learns drift. If the first signal is execution, the day has to organize around that.

## The method

> "Discipline is the decision to make the first move clean."

Start with the action that creates the most downstream order. Maybe that is training, planning, writing, or the one difficult task that prevents the rest of the day from pretending to be productive.

## The mindset

> "The day takes shape around the earliest standard you set."

The first clean action is not about productivity theater. It is about identity under load. You are teaching yourself that a new week starts with command, not reaction.

## Today's call

Before opening the loudest inbox, complete one action that makes the rest of the week easier. Keep it measurable.

Set the standard before the day sets one for you.
