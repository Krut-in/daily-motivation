# Sunday 2026-07-19 · Resilience

> "It is staying with the breaking that produces the blessing."

Sunday is when the week's accumulated weight asks you to quit. The week broke you in small ways you barely noticed at the time. Today's only job is to stay with the breaking long enough for the mark it leaves to be worth carrying.

## The method

> "The choice is yours of what it becomes."

Last week's events are fixed. Your interpretation isn't. Today, take the worst thing that happened this week and write down the most resourceful possible reading of it. Not optimistic. Resourceful. What does this hand you that you didn't have before?

## The mindset

> "Resilience is learning how to bend without lowering the standard."

A difficult week does not mean you are behind. It means you have fresh information about where the system leaks. Fix one leak today and the same pressure hits a stronger structure next time.

## Today's call

Write one adjustment for the coming week and make it concrete: time, place, trigger, or boundary. Then do something that restores your body without numbing your mind.

Tough times never last. Tough people do.
