# Tuesday 2026-05-26 · Focus

> "I want to see what my life would look like if I didn't count the cost."

Tuesday is when the cost-counting begins. Should I really do this? Is this worth the time? Each cost-count is a tiny vote for not trying. Today is the day to stop counting and just go.

## The method

> "Only those who risk going too far can find out how far one can go."

Focus is partly a permission to push past where reasonable people stop. Reasonable is the enemy. The Tuesday version of you that's reasonable is the one who'll never know what the unreasonable version could have built.

## The mindset

> "The biggest enemy you have to deal with is yourself."

External distractions are the smaller enemy. The bigger one is the part of you that finds them. Notice today how often you reach for distraction the moment a hard task gets uncomfortable. That reach is the actual battle.

## Today's call

Block 90 minutes of focused work this morning. Phone in another room. No tabs that aren't the work. When the discomfort hits and you reach for a distraction, just notice the reach and return.

What you give your attention to is the only thing that's actually yours.
