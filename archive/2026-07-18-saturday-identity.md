# Saturday 2026-07-18 · Identity

> "Some of you are a beast until it's uncomfortable."

Saturday's identity test is comfort. The week gave you reasons to perform. The weekend strips them all away. The version of you that shows up only when conditions are perfect isn't actually the version you've been building. Today reveals which one is real.

## The method

> "Identity does not ask what you meant. It records what you did."

Choose one action that keeps faith with the version of you you keep describing. It does not need to be extreme. It needs to be true. A small true action beats a dramatic plan you never touch.

## The mindset

> "If no one thinks you're crazy, you're not yet operating to the outer limits of your potential."

The version of you that scares the comfortable people around you is usually the right one. If your work makes someone uncomfortable enough to call you 'too intense,' that's a signal. Don't soften it. Refine it.

## Today's call

Pick one private rep: a workout, a study block, a cleanup, a hard errand, or ten pages read. Do it without announcing it.

The version of you that shows up at full effort on Saturday is the version that becomes you.
