# Sunday 2026-05-24 · Resilience

> "The pressure in your life isn't crushing you. It's pushing what's on the inside of you out."

Sunday is when last week's pressure either becomes wisdom or becomes weight. The same pressure does both, depending on whether you stay with it long enough to extract the lesson. Today is for the extraction.

## The method

> "Quitting guarantees the failure."

There's a quiet kind of quitting that doesn't look like quitting. It's the slow withdrawal of effort, dressed up as 'this wasn't really for me anyway.' Today, name the thing you've been quietly quitting, and decide whether to actually quit it or actually try.

## The mindset

> "Your character is put to the test when your back is against the wall."

Backed against the wall is just data. It tells you what you're made of, and it tells you what you didn't know you could do. Sunday is the day to face the wall calmly and discover both.

## Today's call

Pick the one thing this Sunday that's been quietly grinding on you. Maybe a decision you've been deferring, or a project you've been letting drift. Pick one and address it before bed.

Pressure that pushes the inside out is the only kind worth staying with.
