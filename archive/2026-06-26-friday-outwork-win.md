# Friday 2026-06-26 · Outwork / Win

> "The difference is often one clean rep when no one would notice a sloppy one."

Friday does not need a speech. It needs one more clean rep. One cleaner handoff, one better set, one finished paragraph, one uncomfortable call. The week is asking whether you stop at acceptable or finish at your standard.

## The method

> "Outwork starts where acceptable effort ends."

Look at the thing you are about to call done. Give it one clean pass. Remove the sloppy edge, finish the missing piece, or make the final decision that keeps it from dragging into next week.

## The mindset

> "Win the finish, not the fantasy."

This is not perfectionism. Perfectionism hides from shipping. Standards improve what is already moving. Keep it moving and make it cleaner.

## Today's call

Choose one deliverable and give it one final honest pass. When it is cleaner, stop and ship it.

One clean rep can change how the whole week lands.
