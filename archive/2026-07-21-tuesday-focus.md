# Tuesday 2026-07-21 · Focus

> "I want to see what my life would look like if I didn't count the cost."

Tuesday is when the cost-counting begins. Should I really do this? Is this worth the time? Each cost-count is a tiny vote for not trying. Today is the day to stop counting and just go.

## The method

> "Distraction rarely attacks loudly. It asks for five harmless minutes."

Make the main thing visible before the day gets crowded. Write it somewhere you will see it. Then defend it with a block of time that is as real as any meeting on the calendar.

## The mindset

> "Cut off anything that doesn't support you. Embed the long-term goal in your mind."

The hardest part of focus is grieving what you're saying no to. Every yes to your one thing is a small no to something else you also wanted. The discipline is letting that grief be the price of becoming who you said you'd be.

## Today's call

Block 90 minutes for the one task that would make the day meaningful. Close every input that is not required for that task.

What you give your attention to is the only thing that's actually yours.
