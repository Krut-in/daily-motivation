# Sunday 2026-06-07 · Resilience

> "When the defining moment comes, either you define it or it defines you."

Sunday is the day this week's defining moments either become yours or own you. Most people let them happen and then build a story around the happening. The disciplined move is to choose your definition first, and let the moments fall into the frame you decided.

## The method

> "Putting the next foot in front of the next foot at least keeps you in the game."

Resilience this Sunday isn't about epic comeback energy. It's about the next foot. The version of you that just keeps walking, even slowly, eventually arrives. The version that stops to wait for inspiration usually doesn't.

## The mindset

> "Two primary choices in life: accept conditions as they exist, or take responsibility to change them."

By the end of Sunday, you've made one of these two choices about this week. Acceptance and responsibility aren't the same as resignation and effort. The disciplined version of you accepts what happened and takes responsibility for what happens next.

## Today's call

Spend 15 minutes this Sunday defining one moment from this week. Write what it actually was, then write the meaning you're choosing to give it. Carry the chosen meaning into Monday.

What you call this week is who you become next week.
