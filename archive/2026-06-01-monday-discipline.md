# Monday 2026-06-01 · Discipline

> "I set the tone for my day. Nobody's going to mess with my day."

Monday's first hour is the most important hour of the week. Whoever or whatever sets the tone of that hour usually sets the tone of the whole week. Today, you set it before anyone or anything else gets a chance.

## The method

> "It's high time that discipline became your best friend. Your daily companion."

Treat discipline less like a punishment and more like a relationship. The disciplined person doesn't fight themselves, they walk with themselves. The morning command isn't a threat. It's an agreement.

## The mindset

> "Without discipline, you're not prepared for the future."

Today's discipline isn't about today. It's about the future version of you who'll inherit whatever shape today leaves them in. Every undisciplined Monday is a small theft from the person you'll be in six months.

## Today's call

Set the tone of your first 90 minutes. Decide it now, before email or anyone else's needs touch you. One specific block of work. Run it before any negotiation.

How you start Monday determines what Friday looks like.
