# Sunday 2026-05-31 · Resilience

> "There's no game without pain. The person who refuses to be denied comes back, again and again."

Sunday is the day you decide whether last week's pain was a wall or a doorway. Same pain, different read. The version of you that calls it a wall stops here. The version that calls it a doorway shows up Monday with more capacity than they had last Sunday.

## The method

> "The choice is yours of what it becomes."

Last week's events are fixed. Your interpretation isn't. Today, take the worst thing that happened this week and write down the most resourceful possible reading of it. Not optimistic. Resourceful. What does this hand you that you didn't have before?

## The mindset

> "Hold on to faith. Things will get better. Those experiences shape you into who you need to be."

Resilience isn't about not getting hit. It's about being a different shape after the hit. Sunday is the day you take inventory of the new shape, even when you're sore from the change.

## Today's call

Take 20 minutes this Sunday and write down: what did the hardest part of this week actually teach me? Not the lesson you should have learned. The one you actually did. Read it before bed.

Pain is data. Refusal to read it is the only loss.
