# Friday 2026-06-05 · Outwork / Win

> "Your survival rate for every last bad day is 100%. You're batting a thousand."

Friday closes the week, and the brain wants to remind you of every hard moment. Every email that landed wrong and every plan that didn't go. But the data underneath is the same as every prior week: you survived all of them. Every single one.

## The method

> "You just begin. You solve one problem, then you solve the next."

Outworking the week isn't about heroic Friday energy. It's about the smallest unit of progress. Solve the next problem, not the whole pile. Friday is the day to remember that the work is just one solved problem at a time.

## The mindset

> "Every time you're going through adversity, it is making you stronger."

Friday's exhaustion is real, but it's not the verdict on your capacity. It's the cost of acquiring more capacity. By Sunday, the soreness becomes the strength. By Monday, the strength becomes the new baseline.

## Today's call

Pick the one task today that the tired version of you would defer to Monday. Solve it before 5 PM. Not heroic. Just solved.

What you finish on Friday is what makes Monday possible.
