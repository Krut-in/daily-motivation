# Monday 2026-05-18 · Discipline

> "If nothing changes, nothing changes."

Monday is the day every plan you made on Sunday meets actual gravity. The version of you that wrote down the goals last night is at war with the version of you who has to act on them this morning. The Monday version wins by default unless you intervene.

## The method

> "Your ego is digging a grave. A grave of comfort you'll voluntarily crawl into."

Comfort feels like rest. It's actually slow surrender. Every time you postpone a small discipline, you're not 'taking it easy,' you're voting against the version of you that wanted something different last week.

## The mindset

> "You have to learn to do when you don't want to do."

Discipline isn't about willpower. It's about removing the negotiation between you and the action. The disciplined person doesn't argue with themselves about whether to start. They just start, and the body follows.

## Today's call

Pick the one Monday discipline you'd be tempted to skip. Maybe a cold shower, or 90 focused minutes before email. Run it before any negotiation begins.

What you do daily becomes who you are eventually.
