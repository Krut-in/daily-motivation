# Monday 2026-05-25 · Discipline

> "You give yourself a pass. You know you should be further in life, but you keep letting you slide."

Monday is when the most generous person in your life shows up to give you a free pass: yourself. You're more lenient with you than anyone else has ever been. Today is the day to be the strict version of yourself you'd never tolerate from anyone else.

## The method

> "Discipline is the ability to give yourself a command, and then follow it."

Make the command precise: not 'work hard today' but 'work on X for 90 minutes starting at 9.' Vague commands get vague follow-through. Specific commands get executed because there's nothing left to negotiate.

## The mindset

> "You can't just have energy when you got energy."

Energy is a result of discipline, not a prerequisite for it. The disciplined person creates energy by acting. The undisciplined person waits for energy that won't arrive without action. The order matters more than the effort.

## Today's call

Pick one specific command for today, with a clear action and a start time. Write it down. Execute it. Don't negotiate.

Discipline is just self-respect with a schedule.
