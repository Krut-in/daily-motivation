# Saturday 2026-05-30 · Identity

> "You become the leader of your space because you're not following anybody. You're innovating a unique path of your own."

Saturday is when comparison gets loud. The week's social feed has fed you a hundred metrics about other people's lives. Today is the day to put all of that down and remember the one thing that's actually yours: your specific path, which only you can walk.

## The method

> "Win or lose, you give every single day everything you have. You leave nothing on the table."

Identity isn't built by checking how others are doing. It's built by asking one question at the end of each day: did I leave anything on the table that was mine to give? Saturday is the cleanest day to answer that honestly because no one else is grading.

## The mindset

> "The more weird you are is a reflection of how committed you are."

If your Saturday looks identical to everyone else's Saturday, you're not moving toward becoming what you said you wanted. The weirdness of your day is a metric. Embrace the slight strangeness of doing your own thing while others coast.

## Today's call

Spend an hour today on something nobody around you is doing right now. Maybe the unglamorous practice, or the meaningful conversation with yourself. Pick one and do it without comparing it to anyone else's Saturday.

The path no one else is walking is the only one that becomes yours.
