# Sunday 2026-07-12 · Resilience

> "Tough times have not come to stay. They have come to pass."

Sunday is the day the week's accumulated weight catches up. Whatever happened Mon-Fri left a residue on you, and the temptation now is to read that residue as a verdict. It isn't. It's weather.

## The method

> "The moment of extreme adversity is the best moment for audacity."

When everything in your week says 'be smaller,' that's exactly when to be larger. The dose-response curve on courage inverts under pressure. The audacious move you delayed is the one Sunday is asking you to take.

## The mindset

> "Your character is put to the test when your back is against the wall."

Backed against the wall is just data. It tells you what you're made of, and it tells you what you didn't know you could do. Sunday is the day to face the wall calmly and discover both.

## Today's call

Take 20 minutes this Sunday and write down: what did the hardest part of this week actually teach me? Not the lesson you should have learned. The one you actually did. Read it before bed.

Tough people last longer than tough times.
