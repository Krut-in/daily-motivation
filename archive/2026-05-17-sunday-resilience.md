# Sunday 2026-05-17 · Resilience

> "It is staying with the breaking that produces the blessing."

Sunday is when the week's accumulated weight asks you to quit. The week broke you in small ways you barely noticed at the time. Today's only job is to stay with the breaking long enough for the mark it leaves to be worth carrying.

## The method

> "The moment of extreme adversity is the best moment for audacity."

When everything in your week says 'be smaller,' that's exactly when to be larger. The dose-response curve on courage inverts under pressure. The audacious move you delayed is the one Sunday is asking you to take.

## The mindset

> "This moment can become your biggest turning point or the moment you lose everything. The choice is yours."

The break itself is neutral. It's just data about pressure. What you do in the 48 hours after the break is the entire story of who you become next.

## Today's call

Pick the one thing this Sunday that the broken version of you would dismiss as too much. Maybe a long conversation, or a project you'd been avoiding all week. Begin it small. Just begin.

Tough times never last. Tough people do.
