# Tuesday 2026-07-14 · Focus

> "You got to pass up on good to get to great."

Tuesday is when 'good' opportunities start crowding out the 'great' one. Every meeting that wasn't on Sunday's plan and every helpful favor for someone else looks innocent on its own. Together they crowd out the work that's actually yours.

## The method

> "Imagine your day only went to 1 PM. What would you have to focus on right now?"

Most days quietly assume you have all 16 waking hours to work with. You don't. You have maybe 4 hours of real focus. Treat your morning as if 1 PM is your hard deadline, and run only what would matter if it were.

## The mindset

> "Your attention is the budget your future spends from."

Focus requires some selfishness with your attention. That is not neglect. It is stewardship. You cannot build the thing you keep donating in fragments to everyone else's noise.

## Today's call

Run one output-first block before consuming optional inputs. Make something before you check everything.

A garden is just a forest someone said no to.
