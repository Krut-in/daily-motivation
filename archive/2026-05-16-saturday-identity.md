# Saturday 2026-05-16 · Identity

> "It is the process of the grind that shapes you and forms you. It's not the game."

Saturday is when you stop performing for the week and become whoever you are when no one's grading. The form of who you are is the cumulative output of the process. You don't get to choose the form, only the process you actually live.

## The method

> "What makes a lion the king of the jungle is its mentality."

A lion isn't born a lion. It earns the title through the hunt, not the kill. Every Saturday you commit to the unglamorous parts of your craft is a day the lion in you stops being theoretical.

## The mindset

> "Commitment is staying true to what you said you were true to long after the mood that you set it in has left."

Identity isn't a feeling on a Monday morning when you set the goal. It's the action you take on a Saturday afternoon when no one would know if you'd stopped. The mood is gone by then. The commitment is what's left.

## Today's call

Pick one thing today that connects to the identity you said you wanted. Maybe a workout no one would notice you skipped, or a page of writing you don't strictly have to do. Do it because it's who you said you'd become.

Whoever shows up on Saturday is who you actually are.
