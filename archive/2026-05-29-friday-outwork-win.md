# Friday 2026-05-29 · Outwork / Win

> "You can't be executing and negotiating simultaneously."

Friday is when the inner negotiator gets loud. The voice asks 'maybe half today?' or suggests 'maybe Monday is fine.' Each negotiation steals from the work. Today, refuse the conversation and just execute.

## The method

> "Every great, successful person was ridiculed before they were revered."

If your work is making someone uncomfortable enough to laugh, you're probably on something. The path from ridiculed to revered runs straight through the part where you keep going while being misunderstood. Today is one of those days.

## The mindset

> "It's not what you perceive about me. It's what I perceive about me."

Other people's perception of your work isn't the data that matters. Your own perception is. Today, take the part of you that's still asking 'is this any good?' and replace the question with 'is this true to what I'm trying to build?'

## Today's call

Pick the one task today where you've been waiting for external validation before going harder. Stop waiting. The validation comes after the work, not before. Run the next 90 minutes as if you already had it.

What you perceive about you is the only verdict that compounds.
