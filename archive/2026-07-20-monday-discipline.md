# Monday 2026-07-20 · Discipline

> "I set the tone for my day. Nobody's going to mess with my day."

Monday's first hour is the most important hour of the week. Whoever or whatever sets the tone of that hour usually sets the tone of the whole week. Today, you set it before anyone or anything else gets a chance.

## The method

> "Excuses sound best to the person who is making them up."

Notice what excuse comes up first this morning, and write it down. Just the words. Tomorrow, when the same excuse arrives, you'll see it for what it is. A regular companion, not a true reason.

## The mindset

> "The day takes shape around the earliest standard you set."

The first clean action is not about productivity theater. It is about identity under load. You are teaching yourself that a new week starts with command, not reaction.

## Today's call

Pick one specific command for today, with a clear action and a start time. Write it down. Execute it. Don't negotiate.

How you start Monday determines what Friday looks like.
