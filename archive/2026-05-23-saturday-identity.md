# Saturday 2026-05-23 · Identity

> "Some of you are a beast until it's uncomfortable."

Saturday's identity test is comfort. The week gave you reasons to perform. The weekend strips them all away. The version of you that shows up only when conditions are perfect isn't actually the version you've been building. Today reveals which one is real.

## The method

> "Get over your feelings. Feelings go up and down."

The mistake is treating your feelings as data about whether to act. They aren't. They're weather. The disciplined version of you acts according to the schedule, and the feelings come along once the action begins.

## The mindset

> "Don't put forth half effort unless you're okay with half results."

Saturday is when you decide whether the identity you wanted is the one you're willing to pay for. Half effort earns half identity. The Saturday rep at full effort costs more, but it's what you actually wanted to become.

## Today's call

Pick one Saturday task where you'd usually give 70% because it's the weekend. Give it the full 110% you'd give on Tuesday morning. Notice how different you feel about it by Sunday.

The version of you that shows up at full effort on Saturday is the version that becomes you.
