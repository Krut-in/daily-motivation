# Tuesday 2026-05-19 · Focus

> "You got to pass up on good to get to great."

Tuesday is when 'good' opportunities start crowding out the 'great' one. Every meeting that wasn't on Sunday's plan and every helpful favor for someone else looks innocent on its own. Together they crowd out the work that's actually yours.

## The method

> "Get rid of the debate. Make the binary code decision: yes or no."

Most lost focus is just unmade decisions sitting in your head. Should I do this? Should I check that? Each unmade decision drains the battery. Convert each one into a yes or no, and the battery refills.

## The mindset

> "A thoroughbred wears blinders so it can stay in its lane and see only the finish line."

Your peripheral vision is the enemy of focus. You don't need to see what others are doing. You only need to see your finish line and what to do in the next 90 minutes to move toward it.

## Today's call

Identify your single most important task today, and put 90 uninterrupted minutes on it before lunch. The rest of the day belongs to whoever wants it.

A garden is just a forest someone said no to.
