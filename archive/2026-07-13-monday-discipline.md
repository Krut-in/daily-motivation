# Monday 2026-07-13 · Discipline

> "You give yourself a pass. You know you should be further in life, but you keep letting you slide."

Monday is when the most generous person in your life shows up to give you a free pass: yourself. You're more lenient with you than anyone else has ever been. Today is the day to be the strict version of yourself you'd never tolerate from anyone else.

## The method

> "Discipline is the decision to make the first move clean."

Start with the action that creates the most downstream order. Maybe that is training, planning, writing, or the one difficult task that prevents the rest of the day from pretending to be productive.

## The mindset

> "You have to learn to do when you don't want to do."

Discipline isn't about willpower. It's about removing the negotiation between you and the action. The disciplined person doesn't argue with themselves about whether to start. They just start, and the body follows.

## Today's call

Before opening the loudest inbox, complete one action that makes the rest of the week easier. Keep it measurable.

Discipline is just self-respect with a schedule.
