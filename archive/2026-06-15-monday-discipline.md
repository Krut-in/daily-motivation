# Monday 2026-06-15 · Discipline

> "The longer you debate the first rep, the more power the excuse collects."

Monday gives your easier self a courtroom. It wants to argue timing, sleep, energy, and whether today is really the day. Do not attend the hearing. Start before the arguments get organized.

## The method

> "Discipline is action taken before mood files an appeal."

The first decision should be too small to debate and too useful to skip. Put your body into motion, open the document, lace the shoes, start the timer. Momentum does not arrive first. It follows the first honest move.

## The mindset

> "A clean start is not found. It is forced."

Your mind respects what you repeat. If you repeat negotiation, it will bring better excuses. If you repeat execution, it eventually stops asking for permission.

## Today's call

Pick one non-negotiable action and do it in the first hour you control. Do not improve the plan first. Execute the opening rep.

Monday is won before the excuse gets eloquent.
