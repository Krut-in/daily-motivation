# Friday 2026-05-15 · Outwork / Win

> "The price you pay for chasing the dream is far smaller than the price you'll pay if you never do."

Friday closes the work week, and the temptation is to coast. The version of you that wants to coast is the version paying the bigger price later. The harder Friday is the one you'll never regret on Sunday night.

## The method

> "I am not phenomenally skilled. I am phenomenally willed."

Talent picks the starting line. Will picks the finish line. Today's question isn't 'am I good enough?' It's 'am I willing enough to keep going past where the talented but tired version of me would stop?' Your answer is one Friday at a time.

## The mindset

> "If you don't have any critics, you're probably not doing anything great."

A Friday with critics is a Friday where you're visible. Visibility means you're moving. The version of you that no one criticizes is also the version that disappears.

## Today's call

Pick one thing today that the comfortable version of you would skip. Maybe one extra hour of work, or one hard conversation you've been deferring. The Friday version of you who chooses it is the Sunday version who won't have regrets.

What costs you everything once costs you nothing forever.
