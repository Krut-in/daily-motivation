# Thursday 2026-07-16 · Belief

> "You don't stop when you're tired. You stop when you're done."

Thursday is when the tired version of you starts asking permission to lower the bar. The reasonable case for stopping looks airtight, and your body and mind both agree. The done version of you doesn't show up until later, but only if you keep going.

## The method

> "Your mind follows the evidence you keep feeding it."

Belief is trained by selecting better evidence. Find one kept promise from this week, even a small one, and let that be the proof you work from today instead of the miss you keep replaying.

## The mindset

> "The mind trusts what the body repeatedly proves."

Belief is strongest when it is honest. You can admit the misses and still refuse the verdict. The ledger is mixed, but it is not empty, and empty is what the critic wants you to believe.

## Today's call

Make one exact promise for the next two hours and keep it. Write it down first so there is no room to rename it later.

Belief is built by the actions belief should have caused.
