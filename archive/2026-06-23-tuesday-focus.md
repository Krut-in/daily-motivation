# Tuesday 2026-06-23 · Focus

> "The main thing does not stay main by accident."

Tuesday is where urgency starts borrowing your attention without collateral. A quick check, a quick favor, a quick detour. The danger is not one detour. The danger is forgetting what road you were on.

## The method

> "Distraction rarely attacks loudly. It asks for five harmless minutes."

Make the main thing visible before the day gets crowded. Write it somewhere you will see it. Then defend it with a block of time that is as real as any meeting on the calendar.

## The mindset

> "Your attention is the budget your future spends from."

Focus requires some selfishness with your attention. That is not neglect. It is stewardship. You cannot build the thing you keep donating in fragments to everyone else's noise.

## Today's call

Name the main thing in one sentence and give it the best 75 minutes you have left today. Protect the block like it belongs to someone important.

Attention is either guarded or spent by strangers.
