# Monday 2026-05-11 · Discipline

> "If you can only execute when you feel like executing, you will lose 100 times out of 100."

Monday is when feeling-based people fall behind. They wait for energy that won't come, blame the timing, and watch the disciplined version of themselves outpace them all week. You're not waiting on a feeling today.

## The method

> "Excuses sound best to the person who is making them up."

Notice what excuse comes up first this morning, and write it down. Just the words. Tomorrow, when the same excuse arrives, you'll see it for what it is. A regular companion, not a true reason.

## The mindset

> "Your mind will always choose the path of least resistance. Train it to choose the path of most resistance instead."

Discipline is the choice your mind makes after you've trained it to choose against itself. It feels alien at first because your default neural circuit is set to ease. Every act of friction is rewiring.

## Today's call

Pick the one decision today that your easier self would skip. Maybe a morning workout, or 90 focused minutes before you open email. Choose it before your mood has a chance to vote.

Mood follows action. The reverse is fiction.
