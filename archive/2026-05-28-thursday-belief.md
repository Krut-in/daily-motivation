# Thursday 2026-05-28 · Belief

> "You don't stop when you're tired. You stop when you're done."

Thursday is when the tired version of you starts asking permission to lower the bar. The reasonable case for stopping looks airtight, and your body and mind both agree. The done version of you doesn't show up until later, but only if you keep going.

## The method

> "If you really want it like you say you want it, you will find a way."

Wanting it is cheap. Showing up tired is the proof that you wanted it. Today's question isn't whether you have energy. It's whether your wanting is louder than your tiredness.

## The mindset

> "The only way out is to step forward and face your fears."

Belief isn't a feeling that arrives. It's an action you take in spite of the lack of feeling. You step forward not because you're sure, but because the only way to find out whether you're capable is to act as if you are.

## Today's call

Pick the one task today that the tired version of you would skip. Do it before you're allowed to call it a day. Not for the result. For the proof.

Belief is built by the actions belief should have caused.
