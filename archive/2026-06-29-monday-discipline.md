# Monday 2026-06-29 · Discipline

> "The disciplined move is usually smaller and earlier than the dramatic one."

Monday drama is optional more often than it feels. A lot of chaos is just delayed discipline with better lighting. Today, do the boring thing early before it becomes the emergency thing later.

## The method

> "Do not wait until the day is on fire to become serious."

Find the task that prevents three future problems. It might be planning, paying, cleaning, training, writing, or sending the message. Handle it before the day has enough noise to make delay sound reasonable.

## The mindset

> "Order is built before urgency arrives."

Discipline is not intensity. Intensity spikes and disappears. Discipline is a quiet prepayment against the version of life where everything costs more because you waited.

## Today's call

Complete one preventive action in the first half of the day. Do not chase drama until the obvious discipline is done.

Early discipline is cheaper than late panic.
