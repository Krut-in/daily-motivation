# Friday 2026-07-10 · Outwork / Win

> "An open loop drains more than it looks like it should."

Friday is loop-closing day. The week left behind loose ends, and some of them are harmless. One of them is not. One of them will tax the weekend and greet you on Monday if you leave it open.

## The method

> "Winning the week means refusing to carry avoidable weight into the next one."

Find the loop with the highest mental rent. Send the message, make the decision, clean the handoff, finish the draft, or write the next action clearly enough that Monday can pick it up without confusion.

## The mindset

> "The finish line is cleaner when you decide what gets closed."

Outworking is not always adding more. Sometimes it is ending cleanly. A clean close is a sign of respect for the next version of you.

## Today's call

Close one loop before the week ends. If it cannot be finished, define the next action so clearly that it stops taking up background space.

Close the loop and take your mind back.
