# Tuesday 2026-06-02 · Focus

> "Where focus goes, energy flows. Clarity equals power."

Tuesday is the day clarity wins. The blurry version of your week with vague goals and half-defined priorities gets devoured by everyone else's clear ones. Today, get specific about the single outcome that has to happen by Friday.

## The method

> "Imagine your day only went to 1 PM. What would you have to focus on right now?"

Most days quietly assume you have all 16 waking hours to work with. You don't. You have maybe 4 hours of real focus. Treat your morning as if 1 PM is your hard deadline, and run only what would matter if it were.

## The mindset

> "If you have scattered focus, you'll have a scattered life."

Your attention is the highest-value asset you spend each day. Most of it gets spent reactively, on whatever pings loudest. The disciplined version of you decides where it goes before anyone else has a chance to ask.

## Today's call

Pick one outcome for this morning that would make the day a win even if everything else fell apart. Block 90 minutes for it before any meeting or message. Treat 1 PM as your hard deadline.

Clarity is just the willingness to choose.
